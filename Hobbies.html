Fitness Obsession

Dating back to when I still was in the final year of high school, I realized that I actually was a little out of shape. Although I was not overweight, but I deeply knew that it didn't look cool. Besides, I always have a profound craving for the ab muscles since when I was young. So I determined my mind to go on a route that I will never regret. Gaining muscles is not easy, neither is keeping low body fat. In this process, I learned about how to control my daily diet, what to eat and what I must not. And those equipments in the gym couldn't be more familiar to me. It is a tranformation of body, but it is more of a transformation of mind. It illustrates my perseverance, while the muscles sculpted on me is the reward. This is my fitness obsession.

I'm a Apple Fan

Me knowing about the products of Apple is literally by chance. In 2009, when I was in middle school, my friend's father gifted my father a device from Hongkong. It came with the name "iPod Touch", which had opened up a new world to me since then. It was quite special, because there wasn't anything like it in the market back then. The design was simply gorgeous and flawless. When I operated it, I was so facinated by it's response speed and overall fluency it offered. Ever since then, I became a fan of Apple, starting to learn its history and culture. And of course, Steve Job is definately the man I worship. All these years, I watched every keynote including World Wide Developers Conferenece and launch of new products. And I experienced the process of Apple improving their services and enriching the capabilities of their products. It is a great company, still innovative even without Steve Jobs. And I believe, someday not far from now, a new product will come along, again changing everything.

More Than Job

Coding is like magic. You type in lines of code, and you get a program doing whatever you want. Despite that bugs can be irritating, that can be eliminated by programmers' patience and constant learning. As soon as I entered this university, I know that I'm gonna pick Software Engineering as my major, because that's where my interest consists in. When I graduate, I will do coding and developing as my job for a living. And I am so fortunate to see it more than just a job, while I can find joy in this thing.
